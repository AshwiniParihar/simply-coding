Hierarchy and Levels:

  • <h1> is typically used for the main title of a page, <h2> for major sections, and so on down to <h6>, which might be used for minor headings.
  • Examples:
    • <h1>: Chapter Title
    • <h2>: Section Title
    • <h3>: Subsection Title
    • There is no <h7>; the hierarchy stops at <h6>.
