                        <li><pre class="tag-highlight">&lt;h3&gt;</pre>: Subsection Title</li>
                        <li>There is no <pre class="tag-highlight">&lt;h7&gt;</pre>; the hierarchy stops at <pre class="tag-highlight">&lt;h6&gt;</pre>.</li>
