Attendance & Participation

Attendance is required for all scheduled classes. If you must miss a class for call outs or anything else, when possible please inform the instructor. We will try to help you catch back up on lab days when needed. Participate actively in discussions, group projects, and coding exercises. This will help reinforce your learning and understanding of the material. While this class is instructor-led, the main responsibility lands on the student to learn, apply, and grow. This class will be different from any other you have done. You have to decide to educate yourself inside and outside of class to be successful.

Assignment & Project Submission

Complete all assigned exercises, challenges, and projects by the due dates. Late submissions may be accepted with prior approval but may affect your overall grade. Capstone projects should reflect your understanding of the topics covered and be presented professionally.

Classroom Behavior

Leave any outside drama at the door. Come to class prepared to focus on learning and making progress. We have minimal teaching time. Let’s make use of our time appropriately. Respectful and professional behavior is expected at all times. Disruptions, inappropriate language, or any form of disrespect will not be tolerated. Collaboration and teamwork are encouraged, but plagiarism is strictly prohibited. Any copied work will result in a review of your standing in the course. We will discuss this in class further, in coding the expectation is different than in other courses. Zero tolerance for complaining. This course is tough. Expect that from day one. YOU CAN DO IT, work hard, and remember you can succeed. You have to choose to be successful and learn in this course.

Appropriate Use of Lab Time

Lab time should be used for self-study, project work, and test preparation. Make sure to use this time effectively to stay on track with the course content and projects.

Earned Certificates

You will have the opportunity to test and earn 3 different certificates that will help prove your knowledge and prepare you for a career in web development.

  • HTML Certificate
  • CSS Certificate
  • Front-End Web Development Certificate

Grading Scale

Your overall grade will be a mix of participation, attendance, and submitted coding projects and assignments. You will be graded against your effort and your improvement. A passing grade will also qualify you for our completion certificate for our course. This is a valuable credential to show future employers of your education and dedication in becoming a world-class web developer.
