1. Click the gear icon (⚙️) at the bottom-left corner of the VS Code window to open the settings menu.
  2. Select "Color Theme" from the options. You can also press Ctrl + K then Ctrl + T as a shortcut.
  3. Scroll through the available themes and select one you like. You’ll see a live preview of the themes as you hover over them.
  4. Click the theme you like to apply it.
  5. If you want more themes, you can download them from the Extensions Marketplace:
    1. Click the Extensions icon (four squares icon) on the left sidebar or press Ctrl + Shift + X.
    2. Search for "themes" and browse the available options.
    3. Click "Install" to add the new theme to your VS Code.
