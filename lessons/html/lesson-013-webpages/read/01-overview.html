Welcome to Multi-Page Websites!

A multi-page website consists of several HTML files linked together, each serving different content or purposes (e.g., Home, About, Contact). Organizing content across multiple pages improves user experience and helps maintain a scalable website structure.

Did You Know? Multi-page websites allow you to break up content into sections, helping visitors navigate easily. For example, having dedicated pages like "About" and "Contact" makes your site more user-friendly!

By distributing content across multiple pages, you can keep your website well-organized, making it easier for users to navigate and find the information they need.
