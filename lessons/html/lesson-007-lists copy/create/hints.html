Homemade Pizza Recipe

  • Ingredients
  • Instructions
  • Notes

Description

Pizza Dough
A basic dough made from flour, yeast, water, and oil.
Toppings
A variety of toppings, including cheese, tomato sauce, and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of warm water
  • 1 packet of yeast
    • Instant yeast
    • Dry active yeast
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • Cheese and toppings of your choice

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Dough
    1. Combine flour, yeast, and salt in a bowl.
    2. Add warm water and olive oil.
    3. Mix until a dough forms.
    4. Knead the dough for 5-7 minutes.
  2. Let the Dough Rise
    1. Cover the dough with a damp cloth.
    2. Let it rise for 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
  3. Assemble the Pizza
    1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
    2. Spread the dough onto a pizza pan.
    3. Add tomato sauce, cheese, and your favorite toppings.
    4. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown.

Notes

  • This recipe makes one medium-sized pizza.
  • Adjust the toppings and cheese to your taste.
  • Try different sauces, like pesto or barbecue, for variety.
