Pro Tip: Use <b> and <i> tags for simple visual styling, but remember that they do not provide additional context to screen readers or search engines.

Note: The <b> and <i> tags are non-semantic, meaning they only change the appearance of the text without adding meaning. In contrast, <strong> and <em> are semantic tags, as they convey importance and emphasis to assistive technologies and search engines.
